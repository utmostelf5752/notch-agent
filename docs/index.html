Also in the box

  • ⌥ space opens it from anywhere — a Carbon hotkey, no accessibility permission needed.
  • Drag files onto the panel or the notch itself, or attach a screenshot from the paperclip menu.
  • A menu-bar sparkle shows it's running and animates while a turn is in flight.
  • Works on notchless displays too — a small black tab is drawn at the top center instead.

Install

  1. Download the DMG and drag NotchAgent to Applications.
  2. First launch: the app is ad-hoc signed, so Gatekeeper blocks a plain double-click. Right-click → Open → confirm, or clear the quarantine flag: 
    xattr -dr com.apple.quarantine /Applications/NotchAgent.app
  3. Hover the notch (or press ⌥ space), pick a provider and a working folder, and say hi.
